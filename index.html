[ What varies ]

No two share a template.

The discipline of the set is difference. Same rigor bar — design tokens, a clipped-line hero, scroll reveals, a reduced-motion fallback, keyboard focus, a per-site build guide — expressed twenty-five ways.

6

Build stacks

Vanilla JS, three.js / WebGL, Astro, Next.js (static export), SvelteKit and Vite + TS — each chosen to fit its product, not a house default.

25

Palettes

From Perigee’s #05060a deep-space black to Farebox’s #ffffff civic white — light themes, near-blacks, burgundy, forest-ink, bleached sand. No accent repeats as a lead.

25

Type trios

Every site pairs a distinct display + body + mono — Fraunces, Anton, Syne, Cormorant, Bebas, Unbounded, VT323 and more. No two trios repeat.

25

Signature techniques

Starfield shaders, self-drawing routes, audio-reactive canvas, molecular models, data globes, CRT pixel dungeons, scroll-synced marginalia — one signature each, never reused.
